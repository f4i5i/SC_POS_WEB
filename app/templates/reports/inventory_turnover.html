Understanding Turnover
  • Turnover Ratio = COGS / Avg Inventory Value (higher = faster selling)
  • Days to Sell = How many days to sell through current stock at current rate

  • Slow (<1x) = Capital tied up too long
  • Dead = Zero sales - consider clearance
